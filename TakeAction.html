Food Rescue

Available anywhere in Canada, any time, our food rescue system connects your generous donation to organisations right in your community.

  • 1st
    Registration
    To start, you have to register on our food rescue system, or give us a call if you would like some more info.
  • 2nd
    Arranges pick-up time
    Depending on your location, amount of food and type of food, our system arranges pick-up of your surplus at a time that works for you
  • 3rd
    Pack up you product
    Depending on your location, amount of food and type of food, our system arranges pick-up of your surplus at a time that works for you
  • 4th
    Pick up product
    A local non-profit / either Second Harvest or one of our partners / arrives at the set time and picks up your product.
  • Done
    All set!
    The organisation brings the food back to their location and distributes it through their food programming.
