Better Bread Pudding

Made with basic pantry ingredients and some day-old bread,

this easy recipe can be served for breakfast or
dessert with milk poured over top or a scoop of vanilla ice cream!

  • Servings4
  • Prep Time10 min
  • Cook Time1hr 30mins
  • Total Time1hr 40mins

The Ingredients

Variation:try with a rich egg bread or a moist white loaf!

The Process

  1. Preheat oven to 350º. Grease an 8x8" baking pan with 1 Tbsp. unsalted butter.
  2. While Heating the remaining 7 Tbsp.
    • Unsalted butter in a small saucepan over medium-low.
    • Stir occasionally, until it begins to brown and smell nutty, about 4 minutes.
  3. After 8–10 minutes, remove from heat and let cool until just warm to the touch.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together with :
    • 3 large eggs
    • zest of 1 orange
    • 2¼ cups whole milk
    • ½ cup (100 g) sugar
    • ½ tsp. Diamond Crystal or ¼ tsp
    • vanilla extract
    • ground cinnamon
  5. Add 6 cups 1" cubed stale brioche (about 11 oz./315 g) to prepared baking pan.
  6. Pour egg mixture over bread. Place baking pan on a baking sheet and bake until top is golden brown, 45–50 minutes.
  7. Let cool slightly.
  8. Serve warm with a drizzle of 1 pint ice cream of your choice, melted, if using.
