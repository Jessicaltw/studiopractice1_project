White Bread

To Do

  • Keep the bread in a cool, dry place
  • Store in paper
  • Store in the fridge (make sure it is sliced.*)
  • Store in bread boxes
  • Only slice when necessary
  • Slice the baked goods before freezing
  • Keep the cut side of your loaf as unexposed as possible

Not To Do

  • Store in plastic
  • Stack other items on top of bread
  • Let sit under direct sunlight or intense heat
